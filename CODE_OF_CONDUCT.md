# Code of Conduct

This project is for constructive, respectful collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Assume good faith, then verify with facts.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and product decisions, not people.
- Welcome contributors with different levels of experience.
- Respect legal, platform, and safety concerns when proposing changes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, PRs, or discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this policy.

If you experience or witness a problem, contact the maintainers privately with links, screenshots, and any relevant context.
